This full denim look explores the resil-ience and strength embedded within femininity. The denim shrug acts as the central element of the outfit, framing the upper body while exposing areas of skin to create a balance between protection and vulnerability. Denim was in-tentionally chosen for its rigid sur-face, reinforcing the idea of strength and endurance within the garment. Inspired by fractured surfaces, scars, and the worn resilience of a balle-rina’s feet, the design reflects the marks of experience carried by the body. The structured quality of the material contrasts with the fragil-ity suggested by these referenc-es, transforming signs of wear and imperfection into symbols of power. Subtle raw edges and distressed details echo the idea of fractures and scars, while the silhou-ette maintains a fem-inine elegance. The piece ultimately cele-brates femininity not only as softness, but as some-thing resilient, shaped by endurance and quiet strength.
