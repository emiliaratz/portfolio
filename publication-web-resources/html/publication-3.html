concept

This windbreaker draws inspiration from or-ganic structures found in nature. The pleat-ed texture of the fabric reflects the delicate ridges of the flower shown on the mood-board, translating natural surface patterns into the garment’s materiality. Voluminous sleeves echo the soft, unfolding shapes of petals, while the off-shoulder neckline en-hances a sense of fragility and femininity. References to water and classical sculp-ture introduce a calm, almost dreamlike atmosphere, where the body appears both protected and exposed. Integrating organic elements is a recurring aspect of my design approach, allowing natural forms and textures to inform contemporary silhouettes.
