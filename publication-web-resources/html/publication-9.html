concept

This tailored suit jacket explores the in-tersection of organic and chemical beau-ty. The design draws inspiration from the iridescent surfaces of seashells as well as the shifting colors of spilled gas-oline, where natural and industrial ele-ments create unexpectedly similar visual effects. The black exterior maintains a sharp, structured silhouette that wraps around the body, while an open back and a small oval-shaped cutout at the waist introduce moments of exposure, soften-ing the form and highlighting the female figure. Inside, an iridescent silk lining reveals shifting hues of blue, pink, and green, subtly referencing these luminous surfaces. The contrast between the strict tailoring and the fluid, light-reflecting in-terior creates a dialogue between control and movement, structure and transfor-mation.
