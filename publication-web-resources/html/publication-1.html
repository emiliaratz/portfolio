concept

This windbreaker explores the relationship between protection and vulnerability through references to reptilian skin and human anatomy. The structured upper silhouette and curved seam lines are in-spired by the protective quality of scales and the natural architecture of the ribcage, creating a shell-like form around the body. Voluminous sleeves contrast with a fitted waist, emphasizing the female silhouette while maintaining a sense of strength and protection. Soft, flowing elements introduce movement and fluidity, bal-ancing the more armored visual ref-erences. The muted green tones and textured fabrics further evoke natural surfaces, po-sitioning the garment between organic growth and constructed protection.
